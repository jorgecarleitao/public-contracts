Designers, we need your help!

As our website gets more attention, our responsibilities increase. We have recently reached the 2.000 daily visitors, and it is part of our responsibility to ensure good practices regarding trademarks.

A user of our website has pointed out that our logo is very close to the official logo of "público", the most subscribed newspaper in Portugal.

By no means we want to misguide users of neither público or publicos.pt and we are thus changing our logo.

Because this project is for everyone and we don't have a budget to hire a designer, we are opening a call for designers, artists and everyone in general to propose a new logo for this project.

What we are searching for:

  • a squared logo in svg format
  • with the same major blue color as the current one
  • related with government openness
  • that we can use as logo of this website
  • preferably clean and simple (like the website is intended to be)
  • submitted until 1st of December

What we offer:

  • that the logo retains the copyright of the author; we only need a licence that allow us to freely use it in the website.
  • A special section in the "about" page with an explanation of the logo by the author, along with its name and link to his webpage.

How will it be decided

If there is more than one proposal, the decision is made by voting (specific rules to be discussed).

Submission process

You can submit your proposal by sending an email to jorgecarleitao@publicos.pt with:

  • An svg of the logo
  • Your name
